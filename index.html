What is CivicHub?

We help you organize services all in one place, make them paperless and efficient, and gain visibility

Go Digital and Leave the Paper Trail Behind

We convert your paper forms and workflows to a seamless digital experience. Simplify your operations and meet the needs of today’s constituents by automating cumbersome paper processes. Our platform will help you deliver government services – with fast, efficient, fully digital solutions that seamlessly integrate with existing forms, documents, workflows, and back-end systems.

Gain Valuable Operational Insights

Get instant visibility into your operations including what are your most popular services, processing time, handoffs, bottlenecks and more. All without requiring staff to change how they work today.
